Monkey D. Luffy

The pirate who dreams of becoming the ‘King of the Pirates’ and finding One Piece.

Here's a time line of Monkey D. Luffy life:

  • 1989 - Born in Foosha Village to Monkey D. Dragon.
  • 1990 - Dragon left Luffy to be raised by his grandfather Monkey D. Garp. Garp put Luffy through various situations to make Luffy strong.
  • 1995 - Luffy was 6 years old when he met Shanks and his crew when they were stationed in Foosha Village. After the crew was in the village for over a year, Luffy expressed his interest in joining the crew and he even stabbed himself under his left eye to prove that he was worthy enough to join the crew.
  • 1996 - Luffy tried his best to convince Shanks to join his crew. In the bar Luffy found the Gomu Gomu no Mi fruit and ate it not knowing that it was a devil fruit. One day when Luffy was sitting at the bar, Higuma's gang came back and Luffy picked a fight with them but they overpowered Luffy. The Red Hair Pirates arrived to fight them but then Higuma threw a smoke bomb to distract them and escaped with Luffy.
  • 2007 - Romance Dawn is where Luffy swears that he will be the Pirate King for the first time in the series. He set out to Shells Town find members for his crew. This is the first island that Luffy sailed to. He met Zoro on this island for the first time and recruited him to join his crew.
  • 2008 Orange Town is a town that is forty-two years old and a place that the Straw Hats crew visited too. Boddle is the mayor of this town and one of the founders. After the rampage that Buggy had caused in this town, Luffy left them with 500,000 berries to help them rebuild their town.
  • 2010 - Arlong Park arc is the time when Luffy, Sanji, Usopp and Zoro had travelled to Cocoyashi Village to search for Nami. this place is ruled by Arlong Park, a fishmen. He leads the Arlong Pirates and Nami is a member of his crew.
  • 2012 - Drum Island is a Winter Island in Paradise. Drum Kingdom is currently known as Sakura Kingdom. It is also the birthplace of Tony Tony Chopper. After Nami falls sick, the Dtraw Hats go to Drum Island to get a doctor. There they met a reindeer doctor Tony Tony Chopper who helped them fight of Wapol.
  • 2013 - Alabasta Kingdom is the desert kingdom in Paradise and it is the fourth island that was visited by the Straw Hats crew. It is where the poneglyph showed the location of Pluton and one of the twenty founding countries of the World Government. They came on this island to help Vivi save her kingdom and get it back from Crocodile.
  • 2015 - Skypiea is an island that is in the sky above Paradise. This island gets introduced in the Skypiea Arc. This is the sixth island that was visited by the Straw Hats Pirates on the Grand Line and the people out here live 10,000 meters above the ground.
  • 2016 - Water 7 is a city in Paradise known for its shipwrights. The Straw Hats crew had stopped here to trade in their gold for food as well as to repair the Going Merry. They also wanted to find a new shipwright for their crew.
  • 2017 - Ennies Lobby is also known as ‘Judicial Island’. It is government-controlled in Paradise. Ennies Lobby is one of the World Government’s three major strongholds and the headquarters of CP9. The Straw Hats had declared a war on the World Government to save Nico Robin.
  • 2018 - Thriller Bark is the arc during which Brook joins the crew and it starts with the Straw Hats travelling to a ghost island where the met Gecko Moria. Moria uses his devil fruit power to try and steal the Straw Hats shadow. With the help of Brook they got their shadows back before sunrise.
  • 2019 - Saboady Archipelago is the last island that the Straw Hats travel to before reaching towards the first half of the Grand Line. They had come to the island to search for a route to the Red Line to get into the New World. Luffy and his crew pick up a fight with the World Nobles.
  • 2020 - Impel Down is the prison that houses various criminals. Luffy had put a hold on finding his crew so that he could go and save Ace so that he doesnt get executed. With the help of Boa Hancock he is able to infiltrate Impel down. He also meets Bon Clay, Buggy, Crocodile and Jinbe and uses their help to get to Ace and escape.
  • 2022 - Marineford Arc is the arc which has had the fans in tears. The arc started with Luffy and his friends failed to save Ace from Impel Down and they all went to Marineford to save him. Even though the odds were against Luffy, he did try his best to save his brother but sadly he couldnt save his brother. After Ace’s death it took Luffy a very long time to mentally recover.

"Luffy possess an immense amount of physical strength that he got through extensive training that he was put through by Garp. Luffy uses his Gears to increase his physical strength and that helps him subdue powerful enemies"
